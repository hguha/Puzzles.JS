WELCOME TO SUDOKU

    To Use:

  • Click on a square in the board(the selected square will be highlighted)
  • Use the on-screen keypad or the numbers on your keyboard to fill in a square
  • Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the selection
  • Click backspace on your keyboard to clear a square

    • Buttons:

  • Check - will check if the current sudoku is solved
  • Solve - will solve the puzzle using backtracking
  • Generate - will randomly create a solvable sudoku for you to try!
